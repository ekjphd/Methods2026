Group Activity: Two Simultaneous Strands

Groups are assigned to either the interview strand or the observation strand.

Interview Strand

Design a 5-question semi-structured interview guide on one of these shared topics:

  • "How researchers in this program decide what counts as data"
  • "How people decide what to share publicly online"

Then interview each other in pairs for 7 minutes. One person interviews; one answers; others observe body language, listen for key terms, or fill out notes.

Observation Strand

Write a detailed fieldnote about this seminar room, right now — without consulting other group members.

Then share and compare fieldnotes across your group. Note: you were all in the same room. What did each person include? What was left out? Why?

Debrief

  • Interview groups: what did the interview surface that a survey couldn't have captured?
  • Observation groups: what did you leave out and why? What does that reveal about your perspective?
Materials
  • Blank paper or shared document for interview guide and fieldnotes
  • Vogt et al. (2012) — interview and observational designs chapters [PDF in Webcourses]
