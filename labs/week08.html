Group Activity: Two Simultaneous Strands

Autoethnography Strand

Write a brief collaborative autoethnographic memo about a shared technology experience. Choose one:

  • A platform you all use (Instagram, TikTok, Discord, Reddit, etc.)
  • The experience of being a graduate researcher in a digital environment
  • The experience of using AI tools in your academic work

Your memo should: describe the shared experience, connect it to a cultural pattern or power dynamic, and reflect on what you as researchers bring to the interpretation.

Discourse Analysis Strand

Apply discourse analysis to a prepared institutional text. [Dr. Johnson selects before class — one of: a platform's community guidelines, an AI company's responsible-use statement, or a university social media policy.]

Your group answers:

  • What does this text naturalize? What assumptions are treated as obvious?
  • What subject positions does it construct? Who is the implied user?
  • What is excluded or made invisible?
  • What power relations does this text enact?
