Group Activity: Design an Experiment

Prompt

Each group receives a research question plausible for a Texts & Technology context. Choose from the suggestions below or use the one assigned by Dr. Johnson:

  • Does annotating a text digitally vs. on paper affect reading recall?
  • Does seeing a content warning change how readers evaluate an article?
  • Does the gender of an author's name affect how readers rate the credibility of an identical argument?

Your Group Must Produce in Writing

  1. The independent and dependent variables in your design
  2. A description of the control condition
  3. One major threat to internal validity in your design
  4. One ethical concern the AoIR guidelines or Markham (2012) would flag
Debrief question: Does "experiment" imply the same kind of knowledge now as when we started tonight?
Materials
  • Vogt et al. (2012), Ch. 3 — When to use experimental designs [PDF in Webcourses]
  • Franzke et al. / AoIR (2020) — Internet research: Ethical guidelines 3.0 [open access PDF]
