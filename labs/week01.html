Tonight's Activity

Orientation Night — No Group Activity

Tonight is for getting oriented, meeting each other, and getting the tools set up. No structured group activity — use the lab time to install and configure Zotero, ask questions about the syllabus, and get to know your colleagues.

Before Next Week Checklist

  • Complete CITI certification (required before Week 2 — see Webcourses for instructions)
  • Read both assigned blog posts (Cosley; Historians' Sketchpad)
  • Come to Week 2 with one written question about experimental research ready to ask
Week 2 is the first student-presentation week. The question you bring is part of your participation for the night.
