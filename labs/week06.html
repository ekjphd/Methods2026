Group Activity: Two-Phase Survey Lab

Phase 1 (15 min) — Critique an Existing Instrument

Each group receives an excerpt from the Rouse & Stanfill (2025) survey instrument on AO3 fandom. [Dr. Johnson to prepare printed or digital excerpts — 5–8 items per group.]

Evaluate the excerpt:

  • Are any questions leading, double-barreled, vague, or loaded?
  • What constructs are being operationalized?
  • What can this instrument capture — and what can it not?

Phase 2 (13 min) — Draft and Critique New Questions

Each group drafts three original survey questions on a technology-related topic of your choosing. Swap question sets with another group and critique:

  • Are these measurable?
  • Would you answer these honestly?
  • What assumptions do they embed?
Materials
  • Rouse & Stanfill (2025) survey excerpt — [Dr. Johnson to prepare from UCF library copy; 5–8 items per group]
  • Vogt et al. (2012) — When to use survey designs [PDF in Webcourses]
  • Vogt et al. (2014) — Coding survey data [PDF in Webcourses]
  • Blank paper or shared document for drafting questions
