Group Activity: Primary Source Analysis

Prompt

Each group receives a different digitized primary source from one of the following collections. Document types to assign:

  • A newspaper page
  • A photograph with metadata
  • A government record
  • A personal letter or correspondence

Suggested sources: Florida Memory · Chronicling America · Digital Public Library of America

Questions Your Group Must Answer

  1. What can this document tell you, and what can it not tell you?
  2. What is absent from this archive and why? Who or what is not represented?
  3. What would a researcher need to establish before treating this as data?
  4. What does Owens's digital preservation framework change about how you'd cite or trust this source?
Materials
  • Primary source documents — [Dr. Johnson to select and distribute before class, one per group from the suggested collections above]
