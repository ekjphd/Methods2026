Dr. Emily Johnson  ·  University of Central Florida

interdisciplinary research methods for curious researchers
About This Course
Program Texts & Technology PhD
Level Doctoral Seminar
Semester Fall 2026
Instructor Dr. Emily Johnson

This doctoral seminar introduces students to the range of research methods used across the six Texts & Technology PhD specializations: Digital Humanities, Digital Media, EPIC (Editing, Publishing, and Interdisciplinary Curating), Public History, Rhetoric and Composition, and Scientific and Technical Communication. Students study experimental, archival, qualitative, quantitative, and computational approaches, developing the methodological literacy needed for dissertation-level research.
